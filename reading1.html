Toph Tucker and Jasmine Lee on Why Restaurant Websites Are Good and We're All Going to Miss Them

(By Meg Miller and Lauren Schwulst)

Review

I feel like restaurants only put effort into creating websites if the sites are used often (e.g., they're a chain restaurant
or their menus change often). I am inclined to feel suspicious of places with overly polished websites since it feels like
they're trying to compensate for bad food. Still, I can definitely appreciate pictures in a menu. However, menus are usually
seen in the restaurant, so it makes sense why websites aren't really cared for.
